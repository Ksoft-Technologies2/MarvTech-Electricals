OVERVIEW

MARVELOUS, the CEO OF MARVTECH COMPANY, was incorporated in 2000. Equipped with the best Electrical converting machines, MARVTECH is currently producing and distributing several lines and sub-brands of electrical materials such as:

switches, Suckets,light bulbs,CCTV and so on

MARVTECH is also very skilled in electrical engineer field, he can help in

Wiring of house, Fixing of electrical pipes, Fixing and Repair of A.C and Referigerators, Installation of CCTV cameras and decoders and so on that has to do with electricity

Due to the quality of its products, MARVTECH has maintained an eminent reputation in the MARVTECH Industry in Nigeria, and its market shares grew constantly and steadily over the past years due to an efficient distribution network and an everlasting commitment to fulfil the customers' needs and meet their every expectations. MARVTECH is constantly on the look out to develop new products that comply with international standards and invests every possible effort into its growth. Our products and services are available in all 36 States in Nigeria and in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja..

MISSION
